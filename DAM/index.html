FOOD SAFETY POLICY STATEMENT

DIEU AVEC MOI LTD,being the service provider in the fruits and vegetables industry in

Rwanda,is committed to consistently provide safe, high quality services to our country by

always satisfying and surpassing their expectations,hence reflecting our competence in

following safe and sanitary working practices through implementation HACCP systems

principles based on RS 184:2023 while observing strict adherence to all statutory and

regulatory requirements and industry's best codes of practice in order to attract customer

requirements from all over the world.

In pursuit of this commitment,the top management shall ensure that this food safety policy

is subjected to continuous review and revision particularly when changes to legislation,

regulatory or technical knowledge occur and always adopt industry's best practices.

The top management accepts its responsibility to maintain a smooth communication with

its customers about all necessary requirements related to effective implementation of

RS 184:2023 and will provide adequate resaurces to continually improve the highly

standarized food practices as well as customer satisfaction.

We will equally expect similarly high standards from our suppliers and contractors.

FOOD SAFETY OBJECTIVES

1.Customer complaint reduction to at least 99% (almost zero complaint) every quarter.

2.To ensure that company staff are quartey trained(four times a year)on food safety and

hygiene.

3.Conduct second party audits(twice a year)to ensure compliance(good practices as per

specifications)
